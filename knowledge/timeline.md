# Timeline

Chronological view of the ideas, papers, and models in the knowledge base, ordered by year. This timeline tracks the evolution of autoencoder architectures (VAEs, CAEs, DAEs) applied to Scanning Tunneling Microscopy (STM) data.

## 2005
*   **September 2005**: Publication of [Universal Algorithms for Learning Theory Part I : Piecewise Constant Functions](papers/binev-2005-universal.md) by Binev, Cohen, Dahmen, DeVore, and Temlyakov in *JMLR*. Introduces data-adaptive partitioning driven by empirical wavelet-like coefficients to achieve minimax optimal learning rates without prior knowledge of regression smoothness.

## 2025
*   **February 2025**: Preprint of [Topological Autoencoders++: Fast and Accurate Cycle-Aware Dimensionality Reduction](papers/clemot-2025-topological.md) by Clémot, Digne, and Tierny. Generalizes Topological Autoencoders (TopoAE) to preserve 1-dimensional persistent homology (PH1) for cycle-aware visualization, introducing a cascade distortion regularization loss and a geometric, near-linear time planar Rips persistence solver.
